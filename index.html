The Patriot Players Drama Club is the Freehold Township High School theater and performing arts club. The club produces two shows each school year: a Fall play and a Spring musical. In addition to the two major shows, the Patriot Players assist in 'Township's Got talent' (formerly known as 'Patriot Idol'), a school-wide talent competition held annually. Interested students can join the club and become a part of a number of different aspects of the productions; including acting, singing, dancing, set-building, set-moving, lights operation, sound operation, and production management.

Why is drama club important in school?

Drama club plays an important role for stimulating creativity in children. It changes students' perceptions. A student can, if only for a few moments, become Another and experiment with various personal choices and solutions. It allows the children to communicate with a lot of confidence.

In every session, children are encouraged to listen to each other's ideas and thoughts and to take turns. These activities allow children to recognise the value of concentration; a skill that is vital in the world outside their home.

Drama makes a lesson more active, engaging and meaningful for students and participants and enhances critical and creative thinking skills, thus help students to formulate and express ideas and opinions.

Reasons Why You Should Join #2
In drama club we have a huge cast who are all amazing individuals. Our cast is full of kind hearted and hardworking students, everyone usually gets along and help eachother out. If someone forgets a step to a dance, or a word to a song, don't be discouraged to ask one of your peers what to do! Everyone is here for the same reason, we all love to be on stage and perfom for our families and friends. We all want to put out the best show we can and have a good time! :)
Reasons Why You Should Join #3
Drama class can benefit any child, teaching them wonderful skills that they can carry through life and put to use in many areas. Drama instills creativity and self expression as well as nourishing the imagination. It can teach a child to 'think outside the box', which is a great skill for success in future endeavours.
